Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our compaction services

What types of waste can you compact?

We can compact most solid waste materials including general waste, cardboard, plastics, and construction debris. We do not compact concrete, masonry, roofing shingles, or hazardous materials.

Will compaction make my dumpster overweight?

90% of the time, our service will not put you over your weight limit. In rare cases where it does, the cost savings from reduced haul-offs still make our service more economical than additional dumpster swaps.

How long does each compaction session take?

Our mobile compaction service takes an average of 10 minutes per dumpster. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your operations.

Do I need to sign a contract?

No contracts required! We offer flexible service options including on-demand compaction and scheduled service packages to fit your needs.

What dumpster sizes do you service?

We service open-top dumpsters in 20, 30, and 40-yard sizes. We do not service concrete or demolition-specific dumpsters.
